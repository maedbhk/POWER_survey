POWER Survey 2023 on Parental Leave and Childcare
==============================

In Summer 2023, a [survey](https://forms.gle/dNrjzGkm4nh189XE8) was sent out to postdocs on the topic of parental leave and childcare policies at MIT. 

The [results](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1VMELasSNFhN1QjrGt7P7uAFt0IQIIH-c8I0jKGWZdzI/edit?usp=sharing) of this survey were shared with the director of postdoctoral services and the director of MIT Daycare in July 2023.
